## 1. All we need is your website URL

... to start the AI powered test auto-discovery. We will discover, run and maintain your UI tests.
All you have to do is enter your website URL.

## 2. We will discover your user flows

After naming your project, at the first set-up, you can select what should we auto-discover:

- a `log in` test
- a `change language` test
- a reachability check for all `links` on your page
- or just go with `only custom` test cases

## 3. Create account

Upon completion of the initial test creation, you will be prompted to provide your email address.

<Frame caption="Create your account to access application, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img
    src="/images/how-octomind-works/sign-up-1.png"
    alt="name your project screen, screenshot 09/2023"
  />
</Frame>

 **Once confirmed:**
- you will be redirected to a page to establish your password
- upon completion, these credentials will serve as your account details for the further use of our application

**You have successfully signed up!**

## 4. Enter the application
If automatic redirection to our app does not occur after confirming your email, manually log in with the previously
set credentials.

## 5. We will generate, host and run your test code
We will generate **Playwright test code**, host and run the tests for you.
Upon your initial login to the application, you will encounter the test that was configured during your
registration process.

You will see the **test results** in the app and in your PR comments if you integrated us into your CI pipeline.
