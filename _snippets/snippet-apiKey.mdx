<Tip>
  You can get your ApiKey from the [octomind landing
  page](https://app.octomind.dev)
</Tip>

Click on `Create and API-Key`.

<Frame caption="Create an APIKey link">
  <img
    src="/images/create-api-key.png"
    alt="Create APIKey link on octomind landing page"
  />
</Frame>

You then will be navigated to our auth provider where you can create a new org-level APIKey.

<Frame caption="Create an APIKey">
  <img src="/images/create-api-key-propelauth.png" alt="Create APIKey screen" />
</Frame>

<div class="mt-8" />

<Frame caption="Copy the APIKey">
  <img src="/images/create-api-key-copy.png" alt="Copy the APIKey" />
</Frame>

<div class="mt-8" />

<Warning>
  You can only copy the APIKey once, so make sure to do that now. You should not
  share it with anyone.
</Warning>

Please add the APIKey to your CI provider as a secret.
