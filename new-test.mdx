It is crucial to be aware that the process of establishing a new test contains `three` essential phases:

- **the creation of the test**

- **its publication**

- **the reporting of the test**

## 1. Create new test

Upon launching the application, you will encounter a button labeled `create test case`.

<Frame caption="Create new test case, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/create-new-test-1.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

After selecting `create test case`, a **new draft** will be opened:

<Frame caption="New draft pop up, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/create-new-test-2.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

Your attention should be directed towards the right section, the new draft. It is required to:
- assign a **name** to it
- select the preferred mode for your test to operate, which can be either **prompt-based, recording** or **manual**
- **flow** you want us to find and test for you

<Frame caption="Closer look into new draft section, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/create-new-test-3.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

Here is a description of the starting points for the test case:

**Prompt** is...

**Recording** is...

**Manual*** is...

Once you have completed those steps, simply scroll down and click the `discover` button.

You have successfully created **new test !**

## 2. Publish test

Following the creation of your test, next step is to **publish** the test.

Your test will be located in the test cases section. Upon completion of the test, you may click on it.

<Frame caption="Test Cases section, repository for your stored tests, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/publish-test-1.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

Your following step involves clicking the `publish` button

<Frame caption="Publishing your test, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/publish-test-2.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

Upon completion, your test will be moved to the **published** section.

<Frame caption="Publish section, repository for your published tests, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/publish-test-3.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

## 3. Test report

You have arrived to the most important phase: viewing the **test results.**

Upon accessing your **published** section, you will find the `generate report` button located
in the top right corner.

<Frame caption="Location where the generate report button can be found, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/test-report-1.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

Upon selecting it, you will initiate the test report process and be redirected to a page where you await the
completion of the test results.

Once finalized, these **results** will be displayed to you.

<Frame caption="Display of test outcomes, screenshot 02/2024">
  <img src="/images/new-test/test-report-2.png" alt="enter your url screen" />
</Frame>

**Congratulations**, you have successfully completed your new test and are now prepared to deploy additional tests!
