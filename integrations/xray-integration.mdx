You can connect Octomind to your **xray test management system** to keep all testing results in one place.
You can also import test descriptions to seamlessly convert manual tests into automated ones.

<Info>Enterprise tier feature</Info>

## Connect to Xray

Go to your organization `settings` and choose the tab `integrations`.

  />
